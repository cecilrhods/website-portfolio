## ‚öôÔ∏è How It Works

1. **Theme Toggle**
   - Click the top-right **glowing circle** button to switch between **light and dark mode**.  
   - Background and text colors transition smoothly.  

2. **Profile Upload**
   - Click the **Upload Your Photo** button in the hero section.  
   - Select an image from your device ‚Üí it appears inside the circular frame.  

3. **Skills Section**
   - Hover over icons for **HTML, CSS, Java** ‚Üí they animate with rotation and scale.  
   - Read the small paragraph describing your abilities.  

4. **Projects Section**
   - Click **Add New Project** ‚Üí a modal opens.  
   - Fill in **Title, Description, Image URL, Live Link, GitHub Link**.  
   - Click **Add Project** ‚Üí the new project appears dynamically on the page.  

5. **Contact Form**
   - Enter **Name, Email, Message** ‚Üí click **Send Message**.  
   - Button shows **loading animation**, then a **success or error popup** appears.  
   - Messages are sent via **EmailJS** directly to your configured email.  

6. **Smooth Navigation**
   - Click any navbar link ‚Üí page scrolls smoothly to the corresponding section.
